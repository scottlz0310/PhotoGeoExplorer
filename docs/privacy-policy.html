Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2, 2026

TL;DR: PhotoGeoExplorer is a privacy-focused desktop application. All your data stays on your device. We don't collect, store, or share any personal information.

1. Introduction

PhotoGeoExplorer ("we", "our", or "the app") is a Windows desktop application that helps you view and explore photo location information on a map. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles your data and protects your privacy.

2. Information We Don't Collect

PhotoGeoExplorer is designed with privacy as a core principle. The app:

  • Does not collect any personal information
  • Does not track your usage or behavior
  • Does not require account registration or login
  • Does not upload your photos or data to any server
  • Does not share any information with third parties

3. Local Data Processing

All data processing happens entirely on your local device:

  • Photo Files: The app only reads photos from folders you explicitly select. These files remain on your device and are never uploaded anywhere.
  • EXIF Metadata: The app reads EXIF/GPS data from your photos to display location information. This data is processed locally and never leaves your device.
  • Settings: Your app preferences (language, theme, etc.) are stored locally at %LocalAppData%\PhotoGeoExplorer\settings.json
  • Logs: Application logs are stored locally at %LocalAppData%\PhotoGeoExplorer\Logs\app.log for troubleshooting purposes only.
  • Map Tile Cache: Downloaded map tiles are cached locally at %LocalAppData%\PhotoGeoExplorer\Cache\Tiles to improve performance and reduce internet usage.

4. Internet Connectivity

The app makes minimal internet connections for the following purposes:

  • Map Tiles: The app downloads map tiles from OpenStreetMap or Esri WorldImagery servers to display maps. These requests do not include any personal information.
  • Update Checks: The app periodically checks GitHub Releases for new versions. This request does not include any personal information about you or your data.

You can use the app entirely offline after the initial map tiles are cached.

5. Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services, but does not share your personal information with them:

  • OpenStreetMap: Map tiles are downloaded from OpenStreetMap tile servers. See their Privacy Policy.
  • Esri WorldImagery: Satellite imagery tiles are downloaded from Esri servers. See their Privacy Policy.
  • GitHub: Update checks are performed via the GitHub API. See GitHub's Privacy Policy.

6. Google Maps Integration

The app provides an optional feature to open photo locations in Google Maps via your web browser. When you click the "Open in Google Maps" link, the latitude and longitude coordinates are sent to Google Maps in your browser. This action is entirely optional and initiated by you. See Google's Privacy Policy.

7. Data Security

Since all your data remains on your device, data security is primarily your responsibility:

  • Keep your Windows operating system and security software up to date
  • Use appropriate file system permissions to protect your photo folders
  • Back up your important photos regularly

8. Children's Privacy

PhotoGeoExplorer does not knowingly collect any information from children. The app is designed for general audiences and does not have age restrictions.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
